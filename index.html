Workspaces and Community



Accelerator is the long lasting dream of SmallWorld ever since we started. It was also one of the core push for SmallWorld to be existed.

Young people need times to experiment with their ideas and knowledge they are being exposed. Often times, they are discourage to act because of a great number of reason. There maybe their family want them to study and get a high paid salary job or something in particular.

We believe that anyone could be the next great innovator, inventor, scientist, entrepreneurs such and such that that might help improve millions of lives. Therefore, we wish to build a space and seed funding for passionate, committed young ideas driven individual to work, test, refined to a certain amount of time of their experiment.

This program we are looking at to start in the end of 2014. Let’s us know if you want to be part of this exciting journey.

Proud Startup Teams

There have been a great number of startups, starting, rising and failing with us for the last 2 plus years. These are the one that still standing and merging to survive and hope one day soon the day will come for them.

Codingate is a software product outsourcing provider for both foreign and domestic clients offering personalized one-on-one service whether meeting clients in-person or through Internet teleconferencing. We are driven to lead the way in the field, inspiring and challenging young talented leaders as they create new and innovative solutions addressing social and business issues.

Khmer Enterprises is a social networking for social enterprises in Cambodia. The goal is to inspire students entrepreneurs to take part in entrepreneurship, inspired by established businessmen portfolio. Khmer Enterprises also having its own started like Bamnang, Daily Fast, and Srey Saart.

Toursanak Adventure is an educational adventure travel that challenge traveler to challenge themselves to brainstorm solution to tickle problem in the community or place they are visiting.

ArrowDot is an electronic labs building a simple home device technology with cost efficiency that apply to developing countries. Beside home devices ArrowDot also piloting a few educational robots that aims to encourage Cambodia students to join innovate new technology that can solve daily needs of everyday lives.

Hero ARC’s mission is to empower individuals to be the hero of their own story

Chenla Media is an online video content and specialized production services provider. Their goal is to provide quality video contents online that inform, enrich, and entertain viewers and to provide professional production services that really fit the clients’ needs, Chenla Media is operating by young and dynamic Cambodians. Our team comprises audio-visual professionals with skills in producing, directing, script writing, camera operation, audio and video editing, subtitling, and media and communication design. While Chenla Media operates as a production company, it sells quality, creativity and trust.

SmallWorld Press A startup publishing company currently incubating at SmallWorld in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. The mission is to empower readers with knowledge through the promotion of reading and literacy in Cambodia

TELLSELA is a newly startup based on providing simple tasks and personal services such as bicycle delivery, home finding service and more

Smallworld Smallband is a music band started by a group of young people that are passionate about music to change perception of the public. One of their song, Khmer Flag, is presently playing virally on internet by mostly schools and universities students both inside and outside of Cambodia.

KhmerTalks is a ideas worth sharing in Khmer language. The goal is to encourage and promote public speaking skills, build and store virtual knowledge online for young generation to learn.

Startup School

We believe in education. We also believe that education is more about experience the things that ones is passionately to learn to better him/herself in whatever way convenience for them.

We believe learning thing from teachers are great, but more importantly put those learning into action that life asks us at some point. We also believe that ones who love to learn, he/she can also be their own teach in a much more effective way.

We encourage people to challenge themselves to learn new things with others or with their own intuition.

We also believe that sharing knowledge is a better way to learn also. Therefore, we are also scheduling program to have skills share events as well as Entrepreneurship Education program tat we aim to host every 6 months to create a much more animated community of entrepreneurs whose might be one day the leader of a new industry that will help millions of lives.
